Tamas Ferencz May 09, 2018 (16:04)

Hyarna Georgia ná tolle Atalantea Earonde, ambaro hyarna *perkoronde. I tolle ná nu Angalnóreo ortúre. *Os koranari *yutuxa néya vinye *marindor tunker nyarror i tollenna to kiryantar. Lá aldar alar i tollesse: aiwi karir *aiwestentar i talmasse, to ondor, nete. Etta i nyarror, i fárie nastor, láner nuhtaine fara ar nake i aiwi, i hinna utúlier amna *nannemie. Quéni yestaner winta hloima i nómessen yasse hentenanter i nyarror, mal koranari quena *exénier tenna aukárier ilye nyarror i tollo.

---
*perkoron: n. "hemisphere"
*os: adv. "around, about, approximately"
*yutuxa: card. "two hundred"
*marindo: n. "settler"
*aiweste: n. "bird's nest" cf. G aidha
*nannem-: v. "disappear"
*xéya-: v. "pass (of time)"

I am just wondering how do you say "fun" in Neo-Quenya? Can we make smth usable from EN. gairuid?

Tamas Ferencz Jan 03, 2018 (09:27)

I am not sure. It is possible that Q would not have a noun directly corresponding to E 'fun' but several words used when talking about different aspects of fun. If you think of fun as amusement, then tyalie comes the closest as it is glossed as 'play, game, sport, mirth' ('sport' itself comes from 'disport' = 'diversion, amusement, fun'). Then there is alasse, lala-, nnn.

Tamas Ferencz Jan 03, 2018 (10:02)

+Ицхак Пензев good question:) Quenya Chat I set up explicitly for chatting about anything in Quenya. WorldWheel is for musing about individual words, either looking for a neologism or just the meaning/structure of an attested word. Koirea Quenya was intended for discussing approach towards NeoQuenya, plans, "policies" etc.
'Discussion' is the default category that is created by Google when you set up any community.
But I certainly don't treat any of these as rigid boundaries; as there is no more than a dozen of us who routinely and actively post here I think we can handle the stream of posts however it is structured.

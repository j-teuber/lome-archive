ܤܡܝ ܦܠܕܢܝܘܤ Feb 06, 2016 (11:33)

+Rick Spell
When asking about a group or faction (especially during wartime): "Are they friendly". The attested starting point is cotya "hostile". -ngotto = ñotto "enemy". Also compare avanótë.

Tamas Ferencz Feb 06, 2016 (12:12)

+Rick Spell
for 'draw' there is an EQ verb teta- "attract", coming from the root TEHE, which also yields tea "straight" (Ihttp://eldamo.org/content/words/word-2852526649.html  -  suspect +Александр Запрягаев will touch upon this root when he analyses TEG/TEK etc.).
From PE17 we also have lelya- "appear (of beautiful things), attract, enchant" (http://eldamo.org/content/words/word-2696870919.html), so you can say i parma lelya nin "the book is attractive to me, I like the book"

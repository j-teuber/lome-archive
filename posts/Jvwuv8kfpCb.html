Lőrinczi Gábor May 25, 2016 (18:56)

I would cheat a little bit. E.g.:

Ú-eiphin sui cae yrch anglennar i annon.
At least (= not less than) ten orcs are approaching the gate.

Ú-velin i negyth, uin, ach hain ú-fuion.
I don't like the dwarves at all (= I do not, as an emphasis), although (= but) I don't hate them either.

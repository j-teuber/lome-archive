Tamas Ferencz Mar 04, 2013 (11:18)

Ana Tol Nenisse

Caurea, caurea
Caurea melda,
Ména ruicalasse
lorie oa.

Tulteas i yulmar,
panyasset témanna
ana tol nenisse
óse merin auta.

Tulteas i lícumar
calyas i fanwa sambe
Caurea mi fenna,
caurea mi huine;

Caurea yo asea,
caurea ve lapatte
ana tol nenisse
merin vile óse.

TO AN ISLE IN THE WATER

by: William Butler Yeats (1865-1939)

SHY one, shy one,
Shy one of my heart,
She moves in the firelight
Pensively apart.
             
She carries in the dishes,
And lays them in a row.
To an isle in the water
With her would I go.
             
She carries in the candles,
And lights the curtained room,
Shy in the doorway
And shy in the gloom;
             
And shy as a rabbit,
Helpful and shy.
To an isle in the water
With her would I fly.

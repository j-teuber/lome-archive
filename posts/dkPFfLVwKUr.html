Paul Strack Apr 16, 2017 (07:08)

I've release v0.5.5 of Eldamo.

This release finishes both PE14 and the early Qenya name analysis. I've also done some cleanup on the XSD schema definitions for inflections, and added the "search by part of speech" feature that Tamas asked for.

For the first time, I feel like Eldamo is approaching completion. I only have five major publications left, PE15-16, PE18-19 and PE21. Normally when I release Eldamo, I still feel like I am less than half-way through. I finally feel like I've crossed the half-way mark.

Of course, "half way" simply means I think I have less than 9 years left to go, so "approaching completion" has to be taken with a (big) grain of salt. Still, I think I can finish the data entry in only another 3 years and focus exclusively on analysis thereafter.

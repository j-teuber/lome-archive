Björn Fromén Dec 23, 2013 (18:41)

Another sleighing song, on a less serious note:

Losses hortala
er-rocco-raxanen,  /1/
ter i palari
voro lálala,
súli valtea
láma nyelleron.
Alasse men ná nortie
si lóme lírala. /2/

Nyelleron, nyelleron,
hlara lindale!
Yé alasse norties
er-rocco-raxanen! -- Yé!
Nyelleron, nyelleron,
hlara lindale!
Yé alasse norties
er-rocco-raxanen!


/1/ raxa 'drag', *'sleigh'
("drag, n.: ....  An overland conveyance without wheels; a rough kind of sledge"  [OED])

/2/ *si lóme 'tonight', cf. si loa 'this year'

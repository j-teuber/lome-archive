Is 'quenya' in technicality an adjective? Because the original term for valinorean tongue was 'quenya-lambe,' unless I am mistaken.

Paul Strack Mar 04, 2015 (07:39)

The primitive form of this word, *kwend(i)ja, does indeed look like an adjective to me, but I think that in the Quenya of the 3rd age it was used only as a noun. There is a separate form Quenyarin (PM/379) that might be an adjective.

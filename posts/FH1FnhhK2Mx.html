Paul Strack Jan 30, 2018 (04:12)

I'm experimenting with a new search page for Eldamo. The beta is here:

http://eldamo.org/beta/content/search/search.html

The major changes are:

1) I am trying to compact the search controls to be more mobile-friendly while still being reasonable on desktops.

2) I've replaced the pagination buttons with an "infinite scroll" that adds more search results as you page down.

3) Because Eldamo no longer treats Noldorin and Middle Quenya words as Sindarin and Quenya, I've added two new language options: "Later Quenya (1930+)" and "Sindarin/Noldorin (1930+)". This is for those who don't want to use the "All Quenya" and "Sindarin/Noldorin/Gnomish" options.

FYI, the font size on the search controls is big (16pt) because otherwise the iPhone does an annoying page zoom when you select a control.

Anyway, I'd appreciate it if folks took a look and let me know what they think, especially if folks can try it out on mobile devices.

Nas íta ringa rá Nárie (sinome Ilngilnóresse). Quetir i *vilwiste[1] *laukantuva[2] otsolamettasse. Merinyes. Telin kate nótime kentamnar tere i otsolametta. *Kentanwe[3] ammelda karaitienya[4] (ara párie Arkastaro lambi:)); mal koamma lá yonda[5] faren, epetai mauya nin kakarra kentanwenya lotarwasse...

--------

[1] n. "weather" see http://middangeard.org.uk/vqp/doku.php/nq:vilwiste
[2] v. "grow warm" cf. ninquinta-
[3] n. "pottery"
[4] perhaps mélakaraitie "favourite pastime; hobby"?
[5] I wonder what the best noun would be for 'room, space, area'

+#Quenya   +#WBYeats

Caimance-lire
 
Aini luhtear
Caimalyanna orte;
Yerne ohtallo
As qualini naice.
 
Mi Menel Eru
Marielyainen raina;
Indorya tyare
Itselohte merya.
 
Foanya tye míqua
An ora nin lave
Ta penuvanyel
Yas vea nauvalye.


A CRADLE SONG
 
THE angels are stooping
Above your bed;
They weary of trooping
With the whimpering dead.
 
God's laughing in Heaven
To see you so good;
The Sailing Seven
Are gay with His mood.
 
I sigh that kiss you,
For I must own
That I shall miss you
When you have grown.


---------

Itselohte: the Pleiades, Sailing Seven (QL)

Síra as vessenya eméniemme i Fitzwilliam *autamardenna mi Cambridge. Tana *autamar lá haura, mal hepe anvanime engwion úme - *tekemmar maitarion ve Renoir, Cezanne, Matisse, Rembrandt, nnn., *kantemmar ló Rodin, ta rimbe exe. Mal i héra induinenemma né i sambi yasse mo kime i *kennassi: *silo *kentánie *mélakárienya ná, polin xíta anda lúme imbi i kalpar, yulmar ta exe kapsar.

*autamar(d-) n. "hall of the past, museum"
*tekemma n. "drawn image, drawing, painting"
*kantemma n. "shaped image, sculpture"
*kennasse n. "pottery, ceramic vessel"
*silo conj. "since, as"
*kentánie n. "ceramics, potterycraft"
*mélakárie n. "favourite activity, pastime, hobby"

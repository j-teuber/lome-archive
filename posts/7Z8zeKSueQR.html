Paul Strack Jun 24, 2017 (06:30)

I've release v0.5.6 of my Elvish lexicon Eldamo.

This version has data from PE15. Of the major publications, only PE16, PE18, PE19 and PE21 remain.

I've also started working on parsing Early Qenya phonetic rules, in preparation for the later Quenya phonology in PE18-19 which I hope to get to by the end of the year. But PE16 is next.

EDIT: I hope to start PE18-19 by the end of the year. I doubt I will finish. Both issues are very dense.

Александр Запрягаев Jan 20, 2016 (08:37)

Why such complications? Starting with the exact match to 'to be renewed', envinyanta ná, there are also much attested inceptives; tankata pa. t. tankante, tankanten 'I turned new again' matches the thought as well. There is the rya-pattern, tankarya/tankasse etc. Using xë makes sense as well.

I don't think there is any difference between ortanie and ortie when the suffix is causative tá; as I see, the first is grammatically proper (tá cannot be split), but it loses position for the second which is easier to use in speech. I wonder why enquantuva not _enquantauva_…

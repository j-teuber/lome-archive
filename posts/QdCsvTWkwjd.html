Tamas Ferencz Jul 22, 2017 (18:47)

Hello Kevin, welcome to the community. What textbook did you download?
The number of people studying Quenya at any time is quite small so it is not easy to find ways to get some "live" practice. What I can suggest is that you write/translate texts - short sentences first, then maybe paragraphs etc. -, post them to places like this one, and there will always be people who are willing to comment on it, give you advice etc.
We also have a Quenya Chat category where you can initiate conversations in Quenya.

An important announcement! After some discussion with +Tamas Ferencz we decided to establish, in addition to our Google+ posts, a discussion chat in Google Hangouts system for the members of our group. Any Eldarin language-lover or poet interested in a kind of more immediate and face-to-face scholarly discussions of Sindarin and Quenya matters, a less formal way of interaction and an ability to share with each other our Elvish language ideas and switching opinions on doubtful and obscure matters of Tolkienian philology is invited to send their requests to join the chat!

Tamas Ferencz Nov 22, 2016 (18:44)

Nér akárie tatallálima emma lossea "helyanweo" hya "hísekúmo" nindatalmasse Rannoch Scotiasse. 'Ta quílenka helyanwe karinwa titta limbaron i karir híse', eques. 'Ta rembalima engwe ya eke men kene ar senyave kenilve sa Anar ná ka elve.'
I erinqua alda i emmo endasse rie kare i emma anvanima.

-----------------------------------

tatallálima adj. "admirable, marvellous, stunning" from tatalla- "admire, marvel at"
hísekúma n. "fog-bow"
quílenka adj. "colourless" quíle "colour" + enka privative suffix
rembalima adj. "captivating, entangling, mesmerizing" from remba- "to net, entrap"
Amazing white rainbow snapped over Scottish moor - BBC News
A stunning shot of a white "fog bow" is captured by a photographer over Rannoch Moor in the west of Scotland.

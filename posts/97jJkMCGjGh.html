Paul Strack May 05, 2018 (07:24)

I've release version 0.6.4 of Eldamo:

http://eldamo.org/index.html

I've continued working on Neo-Eldarin vocabulary, finished roots up through F and beginning on G. I also took a stab at classifying Quenya verbs: this classification can be seen (for example) in the page on the Quenya past tense:

http://eldamo.org/content/words/word-3224058975.html

Finally, I added a "?" (help) button to the search:

http://eldamo.org/content/search/search.html

This documents the search enhancements I've added in the last couple releases for wildcards and multi-search using star (*), comma (,) and plus (+). Other than minor bugfixes these features haven't changed, but they are better explained now.

FYI, in the short term I intended to maintain the current cadences of monthly releases.

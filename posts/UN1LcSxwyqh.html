I've release v0.5.7 of my Elvish lexicon Eldamo.

This version has data from PE16. Of the major publications, only PE18, PE19 and PE21 remain. I've decided to jump ahead and work on PE21 next, since (a) it has a lot of vocabulary and (b) PE18-19 are very dense and will likely take a long time. I hope to finish PE21 by the end of 2017.

Some bits I required.

• kombe 'set' — extrapolation of attested 'gathering, assembly/-age, collection' of PE17:157; especially after the extension okombe is strongly associated with assembies of people.
• nanwa•nóte 'real number' • after Latin.
• valta 'pride' — from PE13 Noldorin blaud as from balát-ā from extension of root BAL: Q valta, T blata, S blaud. Adj. proud, blodren, valtea.

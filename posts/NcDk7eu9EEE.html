Tamas Ferencz Jul 05, 2018 (15:42)

I am happy to announce the release of my NeoQuenya textbook Atanquesta - a NeoQuenya Primer.
The work is aimed at beginner and intermediate students who would like to learn how NeoQuenya works (or, at least, my version of it). It is not a scholarly research of Quenya - for that there are essays and publications available written by people far more competent in analytical linguistics than me.

I would like to thank my friend +Robert Reynolds who has given me tremendous help to iron out inconsistencies and get rid of mistakes, and also to everyone in this community, as you have all shaped and influenced my understanding and approach.

Feel free to distribute the link to the textbook - and I am also very grateful for comments and questions that will hep me to make it even better in subsequent editions.
Atanquesta – A NeoQuenya Primer
Atanquesta is a textbook for those who would like to learn NeoQuenya, a derivative of Quenya, or High Elven, a language developed by JRR Tolkien. Its aim is to present a grammar compiled from Tolkien's ideas that have evolved over the decades he spent working on the language, a grammar as ...

Tamas Ferencz May 29, 2015 (11:55)

Two lesser asclepiads.

Líre nwalca macil , ulle i carne yár,
Alme nyéna ve Fui, naina qualin firimon i fírimon.

Cruel blade sang, the red blood spilled,
Let us weep like the Night, lament the dead man.

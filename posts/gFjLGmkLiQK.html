Александр Запрягаев Apr 28, 2015 (19:16)

A week ago, looking out of the window, I exclaimed:

Lossen i•thîn vi 'Wirith; ringas edlenno, Híril!

[Snowy is April evening; banish the coldness, Lady!] (Oops, the translation is a linnod as well).

edlenna- 'send into exile, banish smb.' < et-LEN-tā (cf. *edlenia- 'go into exile, intr.)

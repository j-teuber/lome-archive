I've released version 0.4.5 of Eldamo.

The major milestone in this release is that I finished analyzing the Ilkorin/Doriathrin languages. This means Eldamo now has two "complete" languages: Ilkorin and Adûnaic. I am also maybe 60% of the way through data entry for PE13, and maybe 30% of the way through analyzing Noldorin names.

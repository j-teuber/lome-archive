A question to discuss. Do you believe that even in nominative Sindarin pronouns are unchangeably lenited?
Is there enough evidence to either side? I personally use them lenited for two reasons:
1) the unexplicable lenition in mae g'ovannen, not really understandable as it would be a non-grammatical leninion of a subject by an adverb which are properly unconnected;
2) the introduction of ho/hon/hono etc. under SE in the Etym, without even giving a possibility of an unmutated version.
In the same vein: how do you deal with the emphatic pronouns? Do you generalise hono to nini, lene or else?

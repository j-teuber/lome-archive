Tamas Ferencz Apr 15, 2014 (13:28)

Manna ta
Ye lá ovanta yo olcar
Hya tare úcarindoron tiesse
Hya hare yo te i *yaiyar,
Mal save alasse mi Héruva axan,
Ar axanerya sana mi aure lómiye.
Nauvasse ve orme orne ara celume empanna,
Ya yave loasse,
Ta yeva lassi la queluvar,
Aiqua care alya.
Laume i olcar!
Nante ve *erdevaine,
Ya súle winta
Potai I olcar lauva termare ter i návie,
La úcarindor mi failaron ocombe.
 
An i Héru tire or i failaron tie,
Nan i olcaron tie nancarenna tulya.

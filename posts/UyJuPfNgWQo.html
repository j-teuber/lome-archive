When in Rome… linnod of the day.

Athar i•Lest ech dorthal, caro be iathrim cerir.

[If you happen to dwell across the Girdle, do as the Fence-People do.]

('On the other side' could be applied to either Doriath or all the lands except it, depending on where the speaker himself dwells. So an -al ending is introduced to show the correct version.)﻿

Tamas Ferencz Aug 28, 2015 (09:40)

Aurissen nolweo néya, í nolwe né vinya, alqua alanye nosseya alquinkion ara passa síreo ráva i liskea nén-talmasse Sutse.

"In the days [of magic long ago] [when magic was yet new], a swan nurtured her brood of cygnets by the banks of a smooth river in the reedy marshland of Sutse."

The first sentence of The Story Of Kullervo by JRR Tolkien.

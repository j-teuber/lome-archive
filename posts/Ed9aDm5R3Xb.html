Josua Del Socorro May 18, 2016 (14:06)

Sí nerim i eil,
teithol i fuin adh Anor,
gin a im, cenedril e galad,
lhaich edaid e naur,
narthannen vi lû dadui a had,

Istassen i eneth gîn,
Istassen i thîr gîn,
i veleth gîn a vless,
io and a thî go-'erir,
erdhyn racthar min enedh,
ú-dharui, i laer linnam...

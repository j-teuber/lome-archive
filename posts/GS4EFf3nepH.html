Robert Reynolds Aug 30, 2018 (13:29)

“Kare munta úkárima: mo lá ista lúme yasse etelyanie”.
“Doing nothing is difficult: you don’t know when you’re done.”

“Avan hanquete tana *maquenta; i tyarma i lá istan hanquenta.”
“I refuse to answer that question on the grounds that I don’t know the answer.”

“Tire ita *latírina tyare ita *latírina tirina?”
“Doesn’t expecting the unexpected make the unexpected expected?”

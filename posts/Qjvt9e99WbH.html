Just a bit of fun - the Middle-Earth version of Nat King Cole's Let There Be Love

Nai euva elye
Nai euva ni
Nai euvar lingwi
Mi earon sí
 
Nai euva vaile
Miste si hya tá
Úmea matso
Miruvor helca
 
Nai liruvar aiwi
Aldingasse
Mo inye mantuva
Yas' hotinye
 
Nai euva tuilindo
Lirulin, ambale
Mal nó illi
Nai euva melme
Let There Be Love - Nat King Cole
